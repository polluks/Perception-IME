Changes to the 常用漢字
The 常用漢字 list has been under review for several years. In July 2008 the 文化審議会国語分科会の漢字小委員会 announced a list of 188 kanji proposed for addition. They include quite common kanji such as 俺 (おれ) and 丼 (どんぶりの"どん"). The full list is:

The decision on this proposal is due in early 2009.
